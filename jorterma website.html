Our Story

Founded in the aftermath of Liberia's devastating Ebola crisis, Joterma Learning Center emerged as a beacon of hope for the children of Margibi County. Our name symbolizes "unity, healing, and progress," reflecting our commitment to rebuilding communities through education.

What began as a grassroots initiative by local educators, concerned parents, and returning diaspora members has grown into a thriving educational institution serving hundreds of students each year.

We specialize in creating nurturing environments where children can recover from educational disruptions while developing the skills and confidence needed for lifelong success.
